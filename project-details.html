Industry: Education

Challenge: Replace rote learning with the practical implementation of theory and analyzing progress in an agile mode

Approach: Techweirdo's POC involves creating a synergy between theoretical knowledge adaptation coupled with goal based implementation. Effective tools to note advice that adapts to a key points indicator for implementation model. Industry: Logistics

Challenge: Increasing the accuracy of costing information

Approach: Inadequate software and absence of technical tools increase human intervention in the costing information process. Businesses confirmed a substantial resistance from accounting personnel to change the current systems. 39% of the supply chain managers that we surveyed confirmed the observations. 88% of the logistics companies who aim to reduce operational overheads are spending almost $223 billions per year on disconnected data systems. There is an over-reliance on external;l financial systems that supply change managers seek to create dependable cost assessment and projection methods. A mere 18% of companies globally have been able to create a difference on this front, which leaves us with an open market for creating a blockchain based model for costing, which is reliable, handles data across systems seamlessly and keeps a track of historical ledgers. The costing information can be created: single, shared record privately or publicly can be stored and accessed by all parties, which will have all the costing data. This takes into account the interoperability of departments where all parties have authorized visibility, reducing the dependency on the third party financial records system much to the supply chain managers delight.
